Visitor Policy The following health condition questions to be asked to all visitors/contractors intending to enter production areas of the factory. Visitors must appropriately signed in, read the GMPâ€™s and be accompanied by management. IN THE LAST 1 WEEK HAVE YOU SUFFERED FROM ANY OF THE FOLLOWING CONDITIONS:
  • Diarrhea or vomiting
  • Salmonella, Campylobacter, Shigella or E.Coli food poisoning
  • Any Parasitic infection
  • Ear, nose or throat infections
  • Skin rashes
  • Recurring boils
  • Typhoid or paratyphoid
  • Dysentery
 If the visitor has any recent history of the above, then contact Quality Assurance for Guidance. Entry to Production areas is subject to the visitor/contractor complying with the following Hygiene rules.
  • Wear Company issued smock and red hair net.
  • Wear beard net if you have a beard.
  • Use antibacterial hand cleanser and hand wash basin at appropriate points.
  • Remove all jewellery and watches except plain wedding ring (must be covered properly).
  • No smoking, drinking or eating (including chewing gum) except in designated areas.
  • No perfume or false nails.
  • All cuts to be covered with a suitable metal detectable plaster.
