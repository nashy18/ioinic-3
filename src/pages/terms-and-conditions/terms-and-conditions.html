Visitors must appropriately signed in, read the GMPâ€™s and be accompanied by management. IN THE LAST 1 WEEK HAVE YOU SUFFERED FROM ANY OF THE FOLLOWING CONDITIONS:
  • 1. Diarrhea or vomiting
  • 2. Salmonella, Campylobacter, Shigella or E.Coli food poisoning
  • 3. Any Parasitic infection
  • 4. Ear, nose or throat infections
  • 5. Skin rashes
  • 6. Recurring boils
  • 7. Typhoid or paratyphoid
  • 8. Dysentery
 If the visitor has any recent history of the above, then contact Quality Assurance for Guidance. Entry to Production areas is subject to the visitor/contractor complying with the following Hygiene rules.
  • 1. Wear Company issued smock and red hair net.
  • 2. Wear beard net if you have a beard.
  • 3. Use antibacterial hand cleanser and hand wash basin at appropriate points.
  • 4. Remove all jewellery and watches except plain wedding ring (must be covered properly).
  • 5. No smoking, drinking or eating (including chewing gum) except in designated areas.
  • 6. No perfume or false nails.
  • 7. All cuts to be covered with a suitable metal detectable plaster.
